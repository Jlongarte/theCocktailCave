Who says great drinks need alcohol? These bold, refreshing, and flavor-packed creations prove that alcohol-free cocktails can be every bit as exciting as their spirited counterparts. Here are 10 non-alcoholic recipes guaranteed to impress.

1. Cucumber Mint Cooler

Ingredients

½ cucumber, sliced
10 mint leaves
25ml lime juice
15ml simple syrup
Soda water

Method
Muddle cucumber and mint. Add lime juice and syrup, shake with ice, strain into a tall glass, and top with soda water.

2. Passion Fruit Mojito

Ingredients

1 passion fruit
10 mint leaves
25ml lime juice
15ml sugar syrup
Soda water

Method
Muddle mint and passion fruit, add lime and syrup, fill with crushed ice, and top with soda water.
